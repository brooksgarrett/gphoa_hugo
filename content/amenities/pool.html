Spending a day outside in the sun won't take you far. Centrally located for the entire community is the pool area. The pool is a spacious sports pool with two tanning ledges. The ledges are different depths allowing one area for small children who need shallow water and a separate area for tanning.

Access to the pool requires a separate membership independent of your HOA dues.
